Our dog, Sparky, likes to eat whatever the kids are snacking on. Is it okay for the dog to eat chocolate?
Chocolate is toxic to dogs. Please do not feed your dog chocolate. Try playing a game with your children - when you feed them treats, they can feed Sparky dog treats.
